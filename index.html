Volta Region, Ghana

The Volta Region is located in southeastern Ghana along the Volta River and Lake Volta, one of the largest man-made lakes in the world. Keta, my hometown, is a coastal city known for its rich history, fishing traditions, and vibrant culture. The region is home to the Ewe people and is celebrated for its beautiful lagoons, beaches, and warm community spirit.
