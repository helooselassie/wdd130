White Water Rafting

At Rapids & Rivers, we believe the river is the greatest classroom on earth. Every paddle stroke teaches patience, every rapid demands courage, and every calm stretch invites reflection. Since our founding, we have been committed to making that experience accessible — safely, sustainably, and joyfully.

History

Rapids & Rivers was founded in 2004 by a group of passionate paddlers who wanted to share the rivers they loved with the world. What started as weekend trips for friends quickly grew into a full-service outfitter, welcoming thousands of guests every season.

Two decades later, our commitment to the river and to the communities that depend on it remains at the heart of everything we do. We partner with local conservation groups, train certified guides, and give back a portion of every booking to river stewardship programs.
