Dry Oar Rafting

Our mission is to provide thrilling, safe, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe in the power of nature to inspire, challenge, and bring people together. Whether you are a first-time rafter or a seasoned pro, our expert guides and top-notch equipment ensure every trip is an adventure of a lifetime.

History

Dry Oar Rafting was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate river guides who shared a love for the wild rapids of the American West. What started as a small operation with just two rafts and a dream has grown into one of the most respected white water rafting companies in the region. Over the decades, we have guided thousands of adventurers through some of the most breathtaking and challenging rivers, always with safety and excitement at the forefront.
