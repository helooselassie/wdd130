Our Purpose

At Rapids & Rivers, we believe the river is the greatest classroom on earth. Every paddle stroke teaches patience, every rapid demands courage, and every calm stretch invites reflection. Since our founding, we have been committed to making that experience accessible safely, sustainably, and joyfully.

We serve first-timers chasing their first splash and seasoned paddlers hungry for a new challenge. Our certified guides bring deep local knowledge, genuine warmth, and an unshakeable commitment to your safety.

History

Rapids & Rivers was founded in 2004 by a group of passionate paddlers who wanted to share the rivers they loved with the world. What started as weekend trips for friends quickly grew into a full-service outfitter, welcoming thousands of guests every season. Two decades later, our commitment to the river and to the communities that depend on it remains at the heart of everything we do.
